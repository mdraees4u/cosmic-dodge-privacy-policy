Privacy Policy — Cosmic Dodge

Cosmic Dodge (“the App”) is a premium, one-time paid mobile game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

Cosmic Dodge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account registration, and we do not request your name, email, phone number, location, or contacts.

The App may store game-related data locally on your device to provide core features, such as:

This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Ads & Tracking

  • We do not sell or share data with third parties.

Permissions

Cosmic Dodge may use device capabilities only to run the game properly:

  • Vibration / haptics (for gameplay feedback)

We do not access your files, photos, microphone, camera, or location.

Third-Party Services

Cosmic Dodge does not integrate third-party advertising or analytics services. The App is distributed via Google Play, which may process purchase and device information as required to deliver the App and handle billing. Please review Google’s policies for details.

Children’s Privacy

Cosmic Dodge does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it.

Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal user database. Local game data remains on your device.
